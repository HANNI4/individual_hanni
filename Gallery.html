MY GALLERY

Photography for me is not looking, it’s feeling. If you can’t feel what you’re looking at, then you’re never going to get others to feel anything when they look at your pictures. What I like about photographs is that they capture a moment that’s gone forever, impossible to reproduce. Sometimes I do get to places just when God’s ready to have somebody click the shutter. The black and white are the colors of photography. For me it's symbolize the alternatives of hope and despair to which mankind is forever subjected.
